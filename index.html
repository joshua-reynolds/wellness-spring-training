Welcome to Spring Wellness Training!


Rules for Play

  1. Every time you exercise for 20 minutes or more from March 1st until March 31st your team gets an at-bat. Record your at-bat.
  2. Your at-bats will be translated, using a random generator, to: an out, a single, a double, a triple, or a homerun.
  3. For every runner that makes it back to home base, your team will get a run on the scoreboard.
  4. Every week, the Beehive League will send a summary of what happened during the week's inning.
